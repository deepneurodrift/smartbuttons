Frequently Asked Questions


What exactly does SmartButton do and does it require a POS?

  • SmartButton is a portable, table-side button. When pressed, it speaks to the guest, captures the request with STT, summarizes it with an LLM, and routes it in order to the right staff. Lastly, no POS required.

How is it installed and what’s included?

  • Setup is simple: buttons for tables connect to a central hub with charging docks. You can self-install or choose an optional technician install.

How much does SmartButton cost?

  • SmartButton offers flexible monthly plans depending on your restaurant size and setup. You can start small with a simple subscription or choose a full hardware + software package. There are also options for self-installation or professional setup with discounts for annual plans.

How does SmartButton improve service speed?

  • SmartButton eliminates the delay between a guest needing help and staff noticing. Instead of waiting for eye contact, guests press the button and their request is instantly routed to the right staff member in priority order. This reduces response time and increases table turnover efficiency.

What types of requests can SmartButton handle?

  • SmartButton can process common requests such as refills, check requests, extra napkins, special assistance, or custom spoken requests. The built-in AI summarizes and categorizes the request automatically before sending it to staff.

Is SmartButton secure and private?

  • Yes. SmartButton only activates when pressed. Audio is processed securely and converted into summarized text, not stored as continuous recordings. Restaurants maintain control over their data at all times.

What happens if the internet goes down?

  • SmartButton includes local fallback routing through the central hub. Basic button alerts will still notify staff even if advanced AI processing is temporarily unavailable.

Can SmartButton integrate with our existing systems later?

  • Yes. While SmartButton does not require a POS to function, it is designed to be compatible with future POS integrations, analytics dashboards, and restaurant management systems.

Is SmartButton suitable for all restaurant types?

  • SmartButton is ideal for casual dining, fast casual, family restaurants, sports bars, and high-traffic environments where staff visibility can be limited. It scales from small cafés to large multi-table restaurants.

How durable is the hardware?

  • SmartButton is designed for real restaurant conditions. It is spill-resistant, easy to sanitize, and built with long-lasting rechargeable batteries to withstand heavy daily use.
